WELCOME TO MYOWNRADIO


The myownradio.biz brings you opportunity to make your own internet radio stream. Just create an account, upload some your favourite sound recordings, send it to your newly created stream and it will be streamed as fast as you turn your stream on!

Project is in pre-alfa, unstable and actively developing state, so if you wanna try it anyway or interested to be a beta-tester, please sign up and welcome!

For any comments, bugs or feature requests please email to info@myownradio.biz.
